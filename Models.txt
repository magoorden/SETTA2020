The content of the models is as follows.

LockIII_base_mode: the model as published in the CCTA paper, yet two errors corrected.
LockIII_model_A: The 'worst' way to model LockIII, yet equivalent in final controlled behavior to the base model.
LockIII_model_B: Model_A with split requirements.
LockIII_model_C: Model_A with split plants.
LockIII_model_D: Model_A with split plants and requirements.
